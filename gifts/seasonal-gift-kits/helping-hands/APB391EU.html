About this kit: Three skin-softening balms for deserving hands
Contents: Resurrection Aromatique Hand Balm, Reverence Aromatique Hand Balm, Eleos Aromatique Hand Balm

Many of our product packaging components are recyclable.

The carton and the insert are made with 100% recycled paper and are recyclable. After use, please consider repurposing the box for at-home storage of treasured keepsakes. Alternatively, please remove these components and place in the recycling bin.

Resurrection Aromtique Hand Balm, Reverence Aromatique Hand Balm and Eleos Aromatique Hand Balm: Aluminium tubes are recyclable and can be placed in the recycling bin. Caps are not recyclable and should be placed in the waste bin.

As recycling practices vary around the globe, please consult local guidelines if in doubt.

PDP How to use

How to use

Apply to clean hands as often as desired, paying close attention to cuticles. Also suitable for drier areas of the body, such as the elbows and feet.

Resurrection Aromatique Hand Balm 30 mL

Citrus, woody and herbaceous with Mandarin Rind, Rosemary Leaf and Cedar Atlas.

Reverence Aromatique Hand Balm 30 mL

Woody, earthy and smoky with Bergamot Rind, Vetiver Root and Petitgrain.

Eleos Aromatique Hand Balm 30 mL

Herbaceous, woody and spicy with Cedar Atlas, Patchouli and Clove Bud.
