PDP How to use

How to use

For luminous, replenished skin, employ each formulation at its recommended cadence.

Lucent Facial Refiner

Twice weekly, either morning or evening, apply a generous layer to freshly cleansed skin, avoiding the eye area. Leave on for up to fifteen minutes then rinse off with warm water. Follow with your preferred Anantara toner and hydrator.

Lucent Facial Concentrate

Massage into freshly cleansed and toned skin, from forehead to neck. Apply any lotions, creams or oils, and sunscreen after, if using. For richer hydration in the evening, blend a few drops with your moisturiser.

The skinin its best light

Two formulations for illumination

New exfoliating mask Lucent Facial Refiner is an ideal preface to Lucent Facial Concentrate, our brightening serum. Both contain plentiful vitamins to replenish the skin.
