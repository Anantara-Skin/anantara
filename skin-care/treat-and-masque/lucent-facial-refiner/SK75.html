PDP How to use

How to use

Twice weekly, either morning or evening, apply a generous layer to freshly cleansed skin, avoiding the eye area. Leave on for up to fifteen minutes then rinse off with warm water. Follow with your preferred Anantara toner and hydrator.

Dosage

One teaspoon

Texture

Lightweight gel-cream

Aroma

Warm, floral, woody

Not your average exfoliation

Refining and replenishing

Lucent Facial Refiners lightweight texture belies its potencywith a combination of PHA and BHA and a suite of vitamins, it spreads easily across the skin in a generous layer.
