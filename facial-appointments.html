Gestures of gratitude Facial Appointments

Meticulous attention to detail, impeccable technique, intense nourishment and exquisite touch: such are the hallmarks of Anantara Facial Appointments.

Call to book a Facial Appointment

A bespoke and immersive
experience


Offered at select locations around the globe, our facials are designed to nurture both the skin and senses. From the moment you enter the aromatic hush of your facial suite, your expertly trained facial therapist is entirely devoted to ensuring you enjoy an enriching experience, for optimal results.

Beginning with a consultation to understand your skin, your appointment will be tailored to address your individual requirements. Facials also include custom-blended formulations to care for specific skin needs.

Post-facial, your facial therapist will provide you with a suggested Anantara skin care regimen for continued pleasure and replenishment at home.

 
 

 

Facials offered

All facials are tailored to suit your skin type. It is recommended you enjoy an Anantara Facial Appointment every four to six weeks, as well as before travel and significant events, and with each change of the season.



45 minutes: streamlined and straightforward 110
Delivers intense nourishment and sensory indulgence in a limited time. Includes a double cleanse, exfoliation, dry skin brushing, custom-blended products applied under steam, extractions or a facial massage, a deep-cleansing or hydrating masque, and a scalp massage; ends with suitable hydration and anti-oxidant fortification.

60 minutes: calming and comprehensive 140
A thorough facial experience for complete relaxation. Includes a double cleanse, exfoliation, dry skin brushing, custom-blended products applied under steam, extractions, a massage of the face, neckline, arms, hands and scalp, and a purifying clay masque; ends with intense hydration.

75 minutes: unhurried and unparalleled 170
An intensely nourishing, all-encompassing facial. Includes a double cleanse, exfoliation, dry skin brushing, custom-blended products applied under steam, extractions, a purifying clay masque, an extended massage of the face, neckline, arms, hands, scalp, lower legs and feet, and a vitamin-rich hydrating masque; ends with a final layer of hydration.


In addition to the times listed, please allow up to 30 minutes for your pre- and post-facial consultation.

Key ingredients and techniques


Our facials employ replenishing, anti-oxidant-rich formulations to help fortify skin that is subjected to the strains of city living, as well as artificial heating and cooling.

Frequently selected ingredients include Vitamin C and Vitamin E, as well as certain botanical extracts renowned for their skin-supportive properties, such as Parsley Seed, Green Tea and Blackcurrant Seed.

Particularly suited to mature skin, chemical exfoliants work to bring balancehelping to address congestion as well as even the skins surface and appearance. Select formulations containing Lactic Acid provide gentle yet efficient exfoliation, assisting with natural skin cell turnover to effect softer, smoother, more clarified skin.

Where needed, a physical exfoliant may also be used for a tailored and more robust approach.

Our range of skin care formulations features a number of vitamins, including Vitamins B, C, D, E and F. Each ingredient is chosen for its ability to calm, balance, nourish or fortify the skin, and address conditions such as dryness, dehydration or depletion.

To address congestion resulting from the day-to-day strains of city living, our trained facial therapists perform extractionsusing only their handsto help clear pores of any build-up of dead skin, excess oil and other debris.

Massage is a fundamental part of the facial experience. Depending on the length of your appointment, you may receive a massage of the face, neckline, arms, hands and scalp to assist with circulation and bring about a state of relaxation.

Spaces of repose

Our facial rooms are uniform only in their serenity.

The design of each is considered and distinctive, their ambience fine-tuned via sound, texture and aroma to ensure effortless relaxation.

The rooms temperature can be adjusted to your preference, and elements such as the diffused lighting, soft bedding and soundproof walls are intended to instil a sense of calm.
