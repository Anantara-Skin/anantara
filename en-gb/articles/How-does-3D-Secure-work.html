How does 3D-Secure work?

Search

Topics

Topics

3D-Secure authentication may be required when completing an online purchase through Anantara.com.

The intention of this extra step is to add a layer of shopper protection by confirming a cardholder’s identify with a unique single-use password, SMS code or temporary PIN.

How 3D-Secure works:

  • Card information is captured when the cardholder enters their payment details.
  • The system will verify whether the card or cardholder is enrolled in 3D-Secure. If you have not enrolled in 3D-Secure, please contact your financial institution for support.
  • Upon confirmation of 3D-Secure enrolment, the cardholder will be directed to a pop-up 3D-Secure page hosted by the card issuer.
  • On the issuer’s platform, the cardholder is prompted to input a unique password or a one-time authentication code, which is either emailed to their verified email address or phone number.
  • Upon successful authentication of the cardholder, they are redirected back to the merchant's website for payment confirmation and the completion of the order.
