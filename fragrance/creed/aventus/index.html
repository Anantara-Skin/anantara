PDP How to use

How to use

Apply as desired to wrists and neck. Throughout the day, the aromatic profile will develop—fresh and sparkling, then warm and rich. Layer with complementary Anantara hand and body care formulations to enhance the aroma.

Top notes

Petitgrain, Black Pepper, Frankincense

Middle notes

Cumin, Cedar, Frankincense

Base notes

Sandalwood, Cedar, Vetiver

Experience Eidesis

Creed Aventus is a woody, spicy and ambery scent that lingers on the body and entrances the mind.

Act I

Rousing aromas of Petitgrain, Black Pepper and a subtle floral accord play across skin warmed by daily ablutions.

Act II

Aromas of ambery Frankincense, spicy Cumin and woody Cedar entrance the nose and those fortunate enough to pass by.

Act III

An aromatic epilogue of Sandalwood, Cedar and Vetiver echoes through the day, leaving behind notes of desire, written in scent.
